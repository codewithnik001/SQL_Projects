# 📊 E-Commerce Data Analysis (SQL Project)

## 📌 Project Overview  
This project focuses on analyzing an e-commerce dataset using SQL to extract meaningful business insights. The analysis helps in understanding customer behavior, product performance, sales trends, and overall business growth.

---

## 🎯 Objectives  
- Identify key markets for targeted marketing  
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior  
- Evaluate product performance and demand  
- Track sales trends and growth patterns  
- Provide actionable business recommendations  

---

## 🗂️ Dataset Description  
The project uses the following tables:

- **Customers** – Customer details (ID, Name, Location)  
- **Products** – Product information (ID, Name, Category, Price)  
- **Orders** – Order-level data (Order ID, Customer ID, Date, Total Amount)  
- **OrderDetails** – Transaction-level data (Order ID, Product ID, Quantity, Price)  

---

## 🔍 Analysis Performed  

### 1. 📍 Customer Distribution Analysis  
- Identified top cities with the highest number of customers  
- **Key Insight:** Delhi, Chennai, and Jaipur are major markets  

---

### 2. 🛒 Customer Engagement Analysis  
- Segmented customers based on order frequency  
- **Insight:**  
  - Customer count decreases as order frequency increases  
  - Majority are **Occasional Shoppers**  

---

### 3. 💰 Product Revenue Analysis  
- Identified products with:
  - Average purchase quantity = 2  
  - High total revenue  
- **Insight:** Premium products contribute significantly despite moderate quantity  

---

### 4. 📦 Category-Wise Customer Reach  
- Measured unique customers per product category  
- **Insight:**  
  - **Electronics** category has the highest demand  

---

### 5. 📈 Sales Trend Analysis (MoM Growth)  
- Calculated month-on-month % change in sales  
- **Insights:**  
  - Highest decline in **Feb 2024**  
  - Sales fluctuated between **March to August**  

---

### 6. 💳 Average Order Value (AOV) Analysis  
- Tracked changes in AOV month-on-month  
- **Insight:**  
  - Highest increase observed in **December**  

---

### 7. ⚡ Product Turnover Analysis  
- Identified fast-moving products based on sales frequency  
- **Insight:**  
  - Product ID **7** has the highest turnover → requires frequent restocking  

---

### 8. 📉 Underperforming Products Analysis  
- Found products purchased by < 40% of customers  
- **Insights:**  
  - Possible reasons: low visibility, weak marketing  
  - Recommendation: targeted promotions & better placement  

---

### 9. 👥 Customer Growth Analysis  
- Analyzed new customers month-on-month  
- **Insight:**  
  - Downward trend → marketing strategies need improvement  

---

### 10. 🔝 Peak Sales Months  
- Identified highest sales periods  
- **Insight:**  
  - **September & December** require:
    - Inventory planning  
    - Increased staffing  
    - Marketing push  

---

## 🚀 Key Business Recommendations  
- Focus marketing efforts on top cities (Delhi, Chennai, Jaipur)  
- Improve retention strategies to convert occasional shoppers into loyal customers  
- Increase visibility of underperforming products  
- Plan inventory for high-demand months (Sep, Dec)  
- Re-evaluate marketing campaigns due to declining customer growth  

---
